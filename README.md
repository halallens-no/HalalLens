## What is HalalLens?

**HalalLens** is a free AI-powered halal food scanner that helps Muslims verify the halal status of food ingredients instantly. Point your camera at any ingredient list, and HalalLens will identify every ingredient and classify it as **Halal**, **Haram**, or **Mushbooh** (doubtful).

### Why HalalLens?

## Features

### Scan & Verify
- **Camera OCR** — Point at any ingredient list, get instant halal analysis
- **Multi-language OCR** — Reads ingredient labels in 50+ languages
- **AI Analysis** — Multiple AI models cross-verify each ingredient
- **Confidence Scores** — See how certain the analysis is

### Ingredient Database
- **Comprehensive** — Thousands of ingredients analyzed
- **E-Numbers** — Complete halal guide for E-number additives
- **Sources** — Every classification includes reasoning and sources
- **Community** — Verified by Muslim scholars and community

### Languages
English, Norsk, Arabic, Indonesian, Malay, Deutsch, Francais, Svenska, Dansk, Turkce, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Dutch
